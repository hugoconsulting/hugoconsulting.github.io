SAP-focused AI agent development & platform services

One connected offering: we build the AI agents, run the platform that powers them, and link both to the SAP systems your business already depends on. Here's how it breaks down.

01 — build

AI Agent Development

Purpose-built agents and generative-AI apps that do real SAP work. We ground them in your data through MCP integration, so they act on live accounting, finance, and master-data context — not guesswork.

  • financeAccounting document suggestions, journal-entry analysis, and anomaly detection over ACDOCA and G/L data.
  • documentsInvoice OCR into SAP-ready formats, plus natural-language summaries and classification.
  • mcpEdge servers that expose SAP data and actions to agents, securely and consistently.
  • multi_modelProvider-flexible setup across leading LLMs, switchable by a single config change.
02 — run

Platform Services

The foundation your agents run on. We stand up and operate the SAP BTP platform, identity, and authentication layers that keep AI workloads secure, governed, and dependable in production.

  • btpCloud Foundry environments, CAP/Node.js services, and app lifecycle on BTP.
  • identityIAS/IPS provisioning, SSO, and role governance across your systems.
  • authXSUAA, OAuth, and principal propagation for secure agent access.
  • operationsAudit logging, transport management, and stable-run support.
03 — connect

Implementation & Integration

The consulting that ties it all together. We handle requirements, design, and integration across S/4HANA and BTP — always requirements-first, so careful scoping and clean implementation stay separate.

  • requirementsStructured scoping and functional/technical design for SAP FI and beyond.
  • integrationOData and RFC integration linking AI to your core business processes.
  • deliveryImplementation, customization, and hand-off docs your team can own.
  • stabilizePost-go-live support to keep new capabilities running smoothly.
